Core Features

Some of of the core features inside the Volet App.

Transfer Money

Users can perform Cashless and Seamless Transactions such as Top Up, Send Money, Receive Money, Pay and Withdraw Money with VOLET by using VOLET Pay or Connect to available banks and directly pay from one Bank Account to another.

Withdraw Money

VOLET allows users to Withdraw money from their wallet to physical cash using Agents that act as VOLET's money exchanger, or, by sending to their bank accounts and withdrawing on a nearby ATM Machine

Set a Savings Budget

Users can set Saving Plans in order to start saving a certain amount of their monthly budget into an untouched account, for a certain period of time, then withdraw to fullfil a certain goal, e.g: A Trip to Bali, or buying a new iPhone.

Limit Daily Spending

Users can set up a limit on which they will spend per day, this way it helps them manage their finances accordingly and avoid overspending. In case you want to purchase something that is above your daily limit spending, a warning will be send.

Split Bill

Users can select to transfer money to one person or multiple people by splitting the bill, evenly or separately, the feature also allows the user to insert tax amount or any extra fees and it will also be deducted between all parties.

Browse Shops

Users can purchase in app services and products, from shops within the app. VOLET allows Merchants to register in the app when they start accepting VOLET Payment, and list their products so that users can see, and purchase products online.
